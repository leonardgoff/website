Leonard Goff

Welcome! I am an assistant professor of economics at the University of Calgary. I mainly do research in econometrics with a focus on treatment effects and nonparametric identification, and topics in labor economics related to nonwage aspects of work and market power.

You can reach me at leonard.goff@ucalgary.ca.

    Some software projects:

    • ivcombine function in Stata for IV estimation with multiple instruments under a relaxed monotonicity assumption
      • This is a work in progress, accompanying the paper: A Vector Monotonicity Assumption for Multiple Instruments.

    • rdbounds function (Stata and R) for bounds in regression discontinuity designs allowing for manipulation
      • This is work with François Gerard, Miikka Rokkanen & Christoph Rothe, to implement the estimator proposed in their paper: Bounds on Treatment Effects in Regression Discontinuity Designs with a Manipulated Running Variable.
      • [Download from GitHub]
